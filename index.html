Our Story

We conducted a survey in the university of Siena regarding the sustainabilty transportation/mobilty awareness and we found out some interesting insights.

So the next step was obvious.

We analysed the survey results and came to conclusions with 3 specific solutions which can solve the issues.

  • <-- Summary of the analysis

With respect to the above, we started 3 campaigns.

1. Awareness of Electric Bicycle facilities by the municipality of Siena to the university students
2. Meme Contest about sustainability for promoting issues among young students
3. Start carpooling as its a real demand

Electric Bike facility by Municipality of Siena

Do you know the municipality of Siena has an affordable electric bike service at a very minimal rate. We found out for you and believe me, this is the most affordable way (as a replacement to walking) to explore/travel within Siena City Centre.
